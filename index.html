Mission & vision

Clean, safe and thriving townships.

Mission. To free communities from poverty and social illness by empowering youth through holistic support that addresses food insecurity, educational gaps, health challenges and domestic instability.

Vision. Transformed townships where generational poverty is broken, every household is stable, and young people have a fair chance to become graduates, artisans, skilled workers and entrepreneurs.

Our programs

Practical support where young people need it most.

Each program removes a barrier that blocks a learner from showing up, learning well and building a stable future.

01

Food & nutritional support

Soup kitchens, school feeding advocacy, food parcels and food vouchers for vulnerable families so learners can focus and perform.

02

Education & career development

School uniforms, stationery, literacy support, tertiary applications, bursary guidance, career fairs and skills workshops.

03

Health, wellness & environment

Sanitary pads, hygiene packs, mental health awareness and clean-up campaigns that restore dignity and protect local spaces.

04

Stable household intervention

Social welfare and psychosocial support advocacy for vulnerable children, child-headed homes and young people facing abuse.

About us

Born from the community it serves.

Founded by visionary teens led by Neo Cholo in Mamelodi, Pretoria East, Teens With Vision is a youth-led community-based organisation shaped by first-hand experience of disadvantaged township realities.

The organisation exists to dismantle socio-economic barriers that limit the educational and economic development of township youth through sustainable, community-driven interventions.
