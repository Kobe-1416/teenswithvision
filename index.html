About Us

Who we are and why we exist.

Our story. Our organization was founded by a group of visionary teens led by Neo Cholo in Mamelodi, Pretoria East. Our organization is a youth-led initiative born from the very communities we serve. Having experienced the challenges of being from a disadvantaged background first-hand, our founders established a CBO.

to dismantle the socio-economic economic barriers that hinder the educational and economic development of the township youth. We are dedicated to fighting poverty and socio-economic hardships through sustainable, community-driven interventions.

Our mission. Our mission is to free our communities from poverty and social illness by empowering the youth through providing holistic support system that addresses food insecurity, educational gaps, health challenges and domestic instability.

Making sure that our townships are clean, safe and consist of a thriving community.

Our vision. We envision transformed townships whereby the cycle of generational poverty is completely broken. Imagine our local townships not as areas of disadvantage, but as vibrant hubs filled with academic excellence and skilled labourers of artisan craftsmanship, where their expertise drive economic innovation and development.

We see a future where child-headed homes are a thing of the past, whereby every household is secure and stable. A future where young people from ko kasi being afforded a real fair chance in life; walking confidently into adulthood equipped with tertiary degrees, specialized skills and being able to build thriving enterprises as entrepreneurs. In this reality, where one is born doesn't dictates their destination and economic uplift and financial freedom is possible for all.

Family & Community Support Read More

Program Story

We advocate for social welfare and psychosocial services to the vulnerable kids, like child-headed homes, or those facing abuse, which gives them the safety net. As clearing the immediate trauma blocking a child's development, we develop a society of responsible functional citizens. As a stable home puts down the heavy burdens of survival and allows one to pick up their books and change their lives.

Problem addressed: household instability, lack of guidance, and vulnerable children needing support.

Food Security Read More

Program Story

The story behind it: As Teens growing in Mamelodi, we saw our own friends going to bed hungry, waking up with no energy to even look at a textbook. As a learner you cannot focus when you are hungry thus even writing an exam on an empty stomach is close to impossible. This leads to obtaining lower grades or even sometimes failing thus one cannot achieve educational prosperity under such circumstances.

The Nutritional Support Initiatives: Fighting hunger through soup kitchens, school feeding scheme advocacy and the distribution of food parcels and food vouchers to vulnerable families. As we believe that feeding our youth gives them the strength to fight for their dreams.

Problem addressed: hunger, food shortages, and the daily pressure of unreliable meals.

Education & Skills Development Read More

Program Story

The story behind it: Talent is evenly distributed but opportunity is not. Many brilliant township kids missed out on higher education because of lack of school uniform, stationery, university application fees or guidance on how to apply for bursaries.

Academic excellence and Career Development: We focus on improving literacy, providing school uniforms and stationery, facilitating assisted tertiary and bursary applications. We also connect the youth to career fairs, bursary fairs and skills development workshops. In doing so this program provides the literal keys to the economy through the efforts to remove academic barriers.

Problem addressed: educational barriers, lack of resources, and limited skills pathways.

Care & Wellbeing Read More

Program Story

The story behind it: It broke our hearts to see girls missing school for a week every month just because they couldn't afford pads or guys losing their way, ending up become drug addicts or commit suicide because of stress and depression or children being sick because of staying near dumping grounds.

Health, Wellness and Environmental Stewardship initiatives: We focus on ensuring dignity through providing sanitary pads and basic hygiene packs, advocating for mental health illness awareness and leading community clean-up campaigns to protect our local environment. As restoring personal and environmental dignity directly impacts a youth's self worth and the confidence they have in claiming spaces and participating in their education and job market.

Problem addressed: health challenges, stress, neglect, and poor wellbeing.
